Getting Started with HomeVue

Installation Guide

Follow these steps to install HomeVue on your device:

  1. Visit the App Store
  2. Search for "HomeVue"
  3. Tap "Get" to download the application
  4. Once installed, open the app
  5. Grant necessary permissions for camera and storage access

Tip: For optimal performance, ensure your device has at least 2GB of RAM and runs iOS 16+.

Room Scan Guidelines

To get the most accurate results when scanning your room:

  • Ensure the room is well-lit to detect surfaces accurately
  • Remove small obstacles or clutter from the floor to avoid scan interruptions
  • Start in a corner and move slowly around the perimeter of the room
  • Hold your iPhone or iPad steadily at waist height for optimal performance
  • Scan walls, floor boundaries, doors, windows and furniture items(if required)
  • Avoid rapid movements or tilting to maintain scan stability and accuracy

Using the Design Tools

HomeVue offers powerful design tools to transform your space:

  • Furniture Catalog: Browse through 3D furniture models
  • Tap & Place: Simply add items into your room view
  • Move: Tap and hold to move items
  • Rotate: Use two finger twist gesture to adjust items
  • Live View: Walk around to see how items fit and look from different angles

Troubleshooting Common Issues

AR Not Working Properly

If you're experiencing issues with AR functionality:

  1. Ensure you have adequate lighting in the room
  2. Check that your device supports AR
  3. Restart the app and try again
  4. Make sure your camera lens is clean and unobstructed
  5. Update to the latest version of HomeVue
  6. Try scanning a room with more distinct features and textures

App Performance Issues

If you notice lag or crashes:

  1. Restart the app
  2. Update to the latest version of the app

Note: Uninstalling the app may delete local data.
